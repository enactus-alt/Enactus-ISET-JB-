Our Impact

HYDROBLOOM

Revolutionizing Water Security in Tunisia

Executive Summary: An ecological initiative targeting Djerba's water scarcity. A revolutionary "bio water filter" that is affordable, biodegradable, and smart.

The Problem

  • Poor Quality: High salinity & heavy metals in tap water.
  • High Cost: Families spend ~500 TND/year on bottled water.

The Solution

A hybrid filtration system combining ancestral materials (Halfa, Clay, Moringa) with IoT technology.

  • Stage 1: Pre-filtration (Cotton, Sand).
  • Stage 2: Purification (Charcoal, Moringa).
  • Stage 3: Remineralization (Oyster Shells, Coral).

Smart Tech: Real-time monitoring via Bluetooth app. Users are notified immediately when the filter needs replacement.

EDUCARE+

Empowering the Next Generation through Sustainable Digital Education

Executive Summary: An innovative educational initiative addressing the lack of awareness of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) among young generations in Tunisia.

The Problem

  • Low Awareness: Limited understanding of SDGs among students.
  • Digital Exposure: Excessive exposure to non-educational content.
  • Lack of Tools: Limited access to interactive civic tools.

The Solution

A mobile-based educational platform combining learning, interactivity, and awareness.

  • Interactive Learning: Gamified modules focused on SDGs.
  • Responsible Digital Use: Encouraging conscious tech use.
  • Smart Engagement: Quizzes, challenges, and progress tracking.
  • Impact Measurement: Dashboards to evaluate awareness improvement.
